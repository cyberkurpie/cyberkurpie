Fennec Fox

Their large ears, which are usually 4 to 6 inches long, help to dissipate excess body heat on hot days.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes zerda
  • Average Length: 24-41 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 14 years
  • Habitat: Sand dunes

The fennec fox, also called desert fox, is native to the Sahara Desert. Its most distinctive feature is its unusually large ears which serve to dissapate heat. Its name comes from this species' name in Arabic: fanak.
